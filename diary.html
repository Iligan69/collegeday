College Day Diary

Asian College of Technology celebrated the amazing events of CCS College Day on September 19. Let us embarck on a journey to look back at the events of such amazing day, and getting to know my personal experiences as well.

Contents

Awardings

To start off the ceremony, the last semester's top students are awarded with their respective rewards. I don't really have a say to this, I'm not a top student ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ Either way, the venue was still peaceful and spaceful, at least during the awarding. See gallery for more awarding images.

Bingo Event

Probably the most chaotic and most populated event that had happened during the College Day. While I struggled to secure a seat, I'm definitely amongst the screaming crowd who had thought that they'd win a round. The suspense of the next would almost make me deaf from the people that are screaming right besides me, but I am definitely one if not the loudest of them all.

Laparoza

The climax of CSS College Day, Laparoza. I am quite stunned of how well they performed. Since most of the performers are my classmates, and seeing how they rehearsed and prepared, I am quite proud of them, and I am not definitely paid to say this. I've imagined how much fun would it be to perform in the venue, but my introvert self would say otherwise.
